our menu popular menu

Affogato

A delightful dessert coffee drink where a shot of hot espresso is poured over a scoop of vanilla ice cream, blending hot and cold elements for a rich, sweet coffee experience.

$8.99

Espresso

A strong, concentrated coffee served in small shots. It’s brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans, creating a rich and bold flavor with a thick layer of crema on top.

$8.99

Cappuccino

A popular Italian drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and frothed milk. It’s known for its creamy texture and velvety foam, often topped with a sprinkle of cocoa or cinnamon.

$8.99

Latte

A smooth, creamy drink made with espresso and steamed milk, topped with a thin layer of foam. Lattes have a more mild coffee flavor compared to other espresso-based drinks, making them a favorite for those who enjoy a softer coffee taste..

$8.99

Flat White

Similar to a latte but with less foam, a flat white is made with espresso and steamed milk. It has a stronger coffee flavor and a smooth, velvety texture.

$8.99

Americano

A simple and strong coffee drink made by diluting an espresso shot with hot water. It’s similar to brewed coffee but has a slightly different flavor due to the espresso base.

$8.99

Mocha

A sweet and indulgent drink combining espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup. Topped with whipped cream, it's a perfect treat for coffee and chocolate lovers.

$8.99

Macchiato

An espresso "stained" with a small amount of milk or foam. It has a strong coffee flavor with just a touch of creaminess, perfect for those who prefer bold coffee with minimal milk.

$8.99
